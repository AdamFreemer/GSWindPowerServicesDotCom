Bent Tree Wind Farm

Wind Capital Group developed the Bent Tree Wind Farm site near the city of Albert Lea in Freeborn County, Minnesota. In early 2009 Wind Capital Group sold the 400MW construction-ready site to Wisconsin Power and Light Co. (WPL), an Alliant Energy Company. The Bent Tree wind farm project area spans over 32,500 acres of land, representing 150 individual Land Lease and Easement Agreements. The wind farm employs 25 individuals directly and indirectly through contracts, bringing positive economic activity to the area. WPL completed construction on the first phase of the project in February 2011. Phase 1 consists of 122 turbines capable of producing 200 MW of energy, which is enough to power 50,000 homes. Phase II is pending due to market needs. When fully-constructed, Bent Tree will have the ability to generate up to 400 MW of emission-free energy.

Open E.I.

OpenEI is growing into a global leader in the energy data realm - specifically analyses on renewable energy and energy efficiency. The platform is a wiki, similar to Wikipedia’s Wiki, with which many people are already familiar. Users can view, edit, and add data – and download data for free.
